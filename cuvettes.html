Technical Specifications Guide

What is the "z-height"?

The z-height is the distance from the base of the cell to the center of the sample window. It is crucial for small volume cuvettes and flow cells with restricted apertures.

Z-Dimension Importance

Essential for positioning small volume cells correctly within the instrument light beam to avoid erroneous or noisy measurements.
